Our Story

A Journey Through Persian Flavour

Nestled within the prestigious Persia Luxury Apartment on 53 Asafoanye O. Broni Circle, Persia Restaurant Ghana brings the timeless traditions of Persian gastronomy to the heart of Accra.

Every dish is a story — crafted from ancient recipes, premium ingredients, and the warm hospitality that defines Persian culture. From slow-roasted kebabs kissed by open flame to fragrant saffron rice and delicate stews, our kitchen is a celebration of one of the world's oldest culinary heritages.

We invite you to a dining experience that transcends the ordinary — where luxury, culture, and cuisine converge in perfect harmony.

Featured Dishes

Signature
Kebab Koobideh

Juicy ground lamb and beef kebab seasoned with onion, turmeric, and sumac, grilled over charcoal flame and served with saffron basmati rice.

Classic
Ghormeh Sabzi

Iran's beloved herb stew — a rich blend of fenugreek, parsley, leek, dried limes, and tender lamb, slow-cooked to perfection.

Popular
Chelo Kebab Soltani

The royal kebab — a regal combination of koobideh and barg (filet kebab), paired with golden saffron rice and grilled tomato.

Beverage
Persian Tea Ceremony

Aromatic black tea brewed with cardamom and rose water, served in traditional glass cups with rock sugar and dried fruits.
